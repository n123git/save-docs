title: Yo-kai
---

Yo-kai begin at the `SectionID` `0x07` (7) at offset `0x20` (32).
  - This can semi-reliably be approximated to the absolute offset `0x5108` 
   
Each Yo-kai entry is has a length of `0x5C` (92) bytes.
